Pivotal HealthWatch

          I led design for HealthWatch, a data analytics product for monitoring cloud platforms. HealthWatch went from unproven concept to gaining 100% customer adoption.
        

Why read: You will learn about my approach to finding product-market-fit, why I created a non-linear user journey map, and how I applied well-established UX design techiques to dog-fooding.
